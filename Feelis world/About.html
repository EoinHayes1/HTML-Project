About Me

I thought it would be fun to do a 20 questions blog and so you can find out a bit more about my interests.

Introduction

Question #1: What do you think about when you’re trying to fall asleep?
Answer: All the things I sniffed on my walk.

Question #2: What’s the first thing you look forward to when you wake up in the morning?
Answer: Going for my walk.

Question #3: What would you buy for someone you really cared about for their birthday?
Answer: A big juicy bone.

Question #4: What’s the latest I could call you at night that you would still answer?
Answer: Any time, I’m always alert.

Question #5: What’s something that scares you?
Answer: The blinds in my house.

Question #6: What’s something unusual that you like?
Answer: Eating ice cubes.

Question #7: What’s actually harder to do than people think?
Answer: Find the best places to mark my territory.

Question #8: What is the thing you’re most thankful for and that you would never want to lose?
Answer: Easy! My favourite chew toy.

Question #9: If you had all the money in the world, how would you spend your day-to-day life?
Answer: I would go for lots of walks, get lots of rubs from my humans and sleep all day. Pretty much what I do in my day to day life now.

Question #10: What is one thing that will always make you smile no matter what?
Answer: Belly rubs.

Question #11: What is your proudest accomplishment?
Answer: That really big hole in the back garden.

Question #12: Who is your best friend?
Answer: Lucca, the yardshire terrier next dog.

Question #13: Do you follow your heart more or your head more?
Answer: I follow my nose and my belly more…

Question #14: What is your favourite TV?
Answer: Spongebob and paw patrol.

Question #15: What is something you wish you had more time to do?
Answer: Play with my best friend Lucca.

Question #16: If you got to choose, what foods would be in your last meal?
Answer: A big lamb bone.

Question #17: What is something that lifts your mood no matter what?
Answer: Going for a walk.

Question #18: Mac or Windows?
Answer: Linux… Duh.

Question #19: Where is one place you'd like to go and what would you do there?
Answer: The dog park.

Question #20: What is something you are glad you did even though you didn’t want to do it at the time?
Answer: Swimming in the sea, I didn’t like the waves at first but I barked at the a few times and they stopped being so scary.
